Introduction

Modern traffic signals often operate on outdated timing plans that fail to adapt to real traffic conditions. This project applies Dutch-style intelligent control using induction loops, radar sensors, and camera-based detection to optimize traffic flow in real time. The result is safer intersections, shorter wait times, and a more efficient commute.

Circuit Diagram

This is a multi-breadboard Arduino Uno circuit designed to control a large number of LEDs using shift registers. It’s a classic example of expanding digital output capabilities with limited Arduino pins.

Environmental Impact Assessment

  • Materials: All electronic components are RoHS and WEEE compliant, minimizing hazardous substances.
  • Energy Efficiency: The traffic-light circuit draws minimal power (~50 mA at 5V), suitable for USB or low-voltage power sources.
  • Reusability: LEDs, sensors, and other modules are fully modular and reusable, reducing electronic waste.
  • Packaging & Shipping: Sourcing all components from a single supplier helps lower the overall carbon footprint.
